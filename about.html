About Me

Courtney is a Brooklyn based interior designer. With roots in a western Canadian upbringing and a love of travel, she brings curiosity and a sense of adventure into her design process. Before deciding to pursue graduate studies in the cultural hub of nyc she earned a bachelor's degree in Art History. She creates multi faceted interiors by layering spaces with color, texture, different styles, and history.

Defining her own personal style as "Bohemian minimal" Courtney also values a critical editing eye. The priority for her in any project she works on is Teamwork and collaboration. Courtney brings a Fun, upbeat, sometimes quirky, and always professional attitude to her teams.

When Courtney's not designing you can find her teaching or practicing yoga, knitting a scarf or dreaming of owning a dog.
