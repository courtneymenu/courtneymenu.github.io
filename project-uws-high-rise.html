Upper West Side Luxury High Rise

About the Project

A-Line Studio was tasked with turning an unfinished studio apartment off the lobby into a new amenity for the building. This new room became a luxe work space that doubles as a private event space.

My Role

I collaborated on the design of this project starting from the initial client interview. I also managed the purchasing and construction and signed off on its completion. From the beginning, our client had a clear opinion on what they wanted the layout and general function to be for the space. We provided guidance and refinement and presented three concept designs for the materials and finishes, refining them down with the input of our client into one cohesive design.

I worked closely through construction with the architect, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, A/V consultant and general contractor hosting weekly and as-needed site meetings to address any issues and to continually assess quality of workmanship. I kept our client informed of the progress throughout this phase.

Project Challenges

One of the most challenging part of this project was integrating the HVAC, sprinklers, recessed downlights, exit signs, emergency lights and A/V components into a low ceiling (it was only about 7’9” to the bottom of the concrete slab above), while still giving the room a feeling of space and height.

We designed the ceiling to include two raised coves with a central chandelier in each and added a gold leaf wallpaper and lit the perimeter with soft LED lighting. We were then able to maximize the ceiling height above the two main seating areas and fit all of the new duct work and sprinklers around the coves to reach all corners of the room. We layered in the lighting and other elements that had to fit concealed within the ceilings.

This was an extremely detail oriented project. Every element of the room was customized and high-end. From the type of stitching on the seams of the custom leather and velvet banquette seating, to hand selecting stone slabs for the bookmatched faux fireplace we did not leave anything out.

Managing the construction of the custom built-in elements, ensuring quality standards were upheld, checking in on the artist applied concrete veneer wall treatments and making sure that the color and texture were consistent with our vision and the client’s expectations. Overseeing the purchasing and delivery of all the high end, custom furniture pieces. Working with each vendor to approve fabrics, finishes and furnishing details and fitting them into the schedule to all be available for the final install date.
